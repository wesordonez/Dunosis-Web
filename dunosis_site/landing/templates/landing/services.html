Data Science and Analytics

Data Infrastructure and Dashboard Development for Stakeholder Insights

We partnered with Swoovy, a startup connecting people to volunteering opportunities in non-profits, to overhaul their data infrastructure. Working closely with their team, we revamped their data systems and developed intuitive dashboards to provide stakeholders with clear insights into funding and financial data. This project empowered Swoovy’s leadership and corporate partners to make informed decisions, driving growth and improving impact.

Bolivia's Migration Trends: Insights into Bolivia's Urban Growth

This Google Data Analytics Capstone Project explores migration and displacement patterns within Bolivia, focusing on the rapid growth of Santa Cruz. Using data from the 2021 Survey for Homes of Bolivia, I analyzed migration trends, socioeconomic indicators, and labor statistics to provide valuable insights for urban planners, policymakers, and business leaders. The project serves as a tool to guide sustainable growth and informed decision-making in Santa Cruz and beyond.

CommuniData-Humboldt Park: Empowering Communities Through Data

CommuniData is an innovative data visualization dashboard designed for community organizations in Chicago. It provides a user-friendly platform for analyzing critical data related to community development. From tracking crime rates to monitoring demographic trends and community resources, CommuniData equips organizations with the tools to visualize, explore, and gain valuable insights. With this powerful tool, community leaders can make informed decisions and communicate their findings effectively, driving positive change and fostering growth within their communities.

Process

Our streamlined process typically takes 2-5 weeks, guiding you from initial consultation to final launch. We begin with understanding your unique needs, followed by design, development, and testing phases. Throughout, we remain flexible and responsive, ensuring your project is under constant revision and improvement for the best results.

1. Initial Consult

We start by getting to know your business, goals, and vision. This consultation helps us understand your needs and ensures we're aligned on the project direction from day one.

2. Design Review

Next, we present initial design concepts based on our consult, ensuring the visual and functional aspects of your website are tailored to your brand and objectives.

3. Design Approval

Once you’re happy with the design, we move forward with your approval. This ensures everything is locked in before development begins, reducing any back-and-forth later.

4. Landing Page

We start by developing your custom landing page, providing you with an initial working version of your website to establish the look and feel, and gather early feedback.

5. Continuous Improvement

We fine-tune your website based on your feedback, making improvements and adjustments to ensure it’s exactly how you envisioned it.

6. SEO And Analytics

We integrate SEO best practices and set up analytics tools, ensuring your site is optimized for search engines and ready to track user interactions and performance.

7. Testing And Refinement

In this stage, we test the functionality, performance, and responsiveness of your website across different devices and browsers, refining any issues along the way.

8. Training And Handoff

Finally, we provide training on how to manage and update your new website. Once you're comfortable, we hand over the reins, but we’re always available for future support!
