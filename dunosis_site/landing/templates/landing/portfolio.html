CP & Advisors

This project involved developing a professional website for CP&Advisors, an accounting firm in St. Louis dedicated to serving the Latino community. The website features a Home, About, Services, and Blog page, providing potential clients with an easy-to-navigate platform to learn more about the firm’s services. The site emphasizes the firm’s commitment to the community, offering bilingual support and tailored accounting solutions for local businesses and individuals.

CommuniData

CommuniData is a robust website designed to provide community organizations with access to essential data dashboards. The platform includes a login feature that allows users to access relevant community data on various topics, including crime rates, demographic trends, and resource distribution. The intuitive interface ensures that community leaders can analyze and visualize data to make informed decisions that drive positive change in their communities.

¡WEPA! Mercado del Pueblo

For WEPA, a small business incubator based in Chicago, this project created a comprehensive informational page that highlights the incubator’s mission and services. The page offers resources for local entrepreneurs and startups, detailing WEPA’s commitment to fostering economic growth in the Latino community. The site serves as a key resource for potential participants looking to learn more about WEPA’s initiatives and programs.

Midwest Untouchable Graphics

The MUG project is an e-commerce platform dedicated to selling handcrafted, Puerto Rican-themed artisanal products. The site is fully integrated with Stripe payments for secure transactions and a shipping system to handle order fulfillment seamlessly. The platform provides a beautiful and functional shopping experience, allowing customers to purchase unique cultural items while supporting local artisans.

Data Science and Analysis Portfolio

Data Infrastructure and Dashboard Development for Stakeholder Insights

We partnered with Swoovy, a startup connecting people to volunteering opportunities in non-profits, to overhaul their data infrastructure. Working closely with their team, we revamped their data systems and developed intuitive dashboards to provide stakeholders with clear insights into funding and financial data. This project empowered Swoovy’s leadership and corporate partners to make informed decisions, driving growth and improving impact.

Bolivia's Migration Trends: Insights into Bolivia's Urban Growth

This Google Data Analytics Capstone Project explores migration and displacement patterns within Bolivia, focusing on the rapid growth of Santa Cruz. Using data from the 2021 Survey for Homes of Bolivia, I analyzed migration trends, socioeconomic indicators, and labor statistics to provide valuable insights for urban planners, policymakers, and business leaders. The project serves as a tool to guide sustainable growth and informed decision-making in Santa Cruz and beyond.

CommuniData-Humboldt Park: Empowering Communities Through Data

CommuniData is an innovative data visualization dashboard designed for community organizations in Chicago. It provides a user-friendly platform for analyzing critical data related to community development. From tracking crime rates to monitoring demographic trends and community resources, CommuniData equips organizations with the tools to visualize, explore, and gain valuable insights. With this powerful tool, community leaders can make informed decisions and communicate their findings effectively, driving positive change and fostering growth within their communities.
